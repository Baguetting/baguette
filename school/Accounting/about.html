About Us

We are Bake-A-Bread. What we do is nice and simple! Since 1887, our brand has been passed down through generations so that you can have some of this amazing bread. We have always been committed to delighting our customers, shareholders and partners, including our senior associates 5x Ballon d’Or winner Christiano Ronaldo, 7x Champion of the World Sir Lewis Hamilton and former five-time World Chess Champion, current four-time World Rapid Chess Champion and six-time World Blitz Chess Champion Magnus Carlsen and many more. We’ve been baking the same way for 140 years to give you our signature delightful experience. Because you don’t just deserve butter, you deserve the best.
