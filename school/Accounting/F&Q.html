F&Q

How does Bake-A-Bread work?
Our Bake-A-Bread Bakers would be ecstatic to walk you through the baking process upon arrival, with no booking necessary. At our prestigious bakery in the Melbourne CBD, you’ll choose from our large variety of baked fictional characters! We pride ourselves on our signature bread, Spider-Bread, which you can purchase from our store in the Melbourne CBD and enjoy the wheaty, lush, golden delicacy straight from our sensational ovens and enjoy the Breadtastic taste!
How much does do Breads cost?
Each Bread costs a different amount based on the intricate design process that went into its creation and the size of the bread. Either way, you’ll be paying a justifiable amount for the mouth-watering tastes you’ll experience from our delicacies.
How long does it take to bake a Bread?
Our ovens heat up the bread to 180 degrees Celsius. This is where our secret baking formula sprinkles the bread magic all over our delicacies. The secret bread-baking formula allows our bread to be a step up from the rest and makes our bread an experience you’ll never forget.
What are the ingredients in the Breads?
Wheat Flour, Water, Iodised Salt, Yeast, Vegetable Oil (Canola), Soy Flour and potentially others depending on the specific bread.
Do you do custom orders?
We do not take custom orders at Bake-A-Bread. This is because the process of handcrafting a magnificent bread design takes a lot of time and labor. We want to ensure that everyone who purchases our products gets the finest experience possible, which is why we have a dedicated menu that you can select and buy from.
Where are we located?
Our single store is located in the Melbourne CBD at 275 Swanston St, Melbourne VIC 3000.
